Policies

health policies

  • Abortion

    Life is a very precious gift and I feel it should be protected so, I am Pro-Life with a few exceptions: Rape, Incest, Protecting the Life of the Mother and in some serious cases birth defects.

  • Medicare-for-all/for-all-who-want-it

    I feel as though this would cripple the economy and the United States as a whole especially, the health-care industry. Health Professionals salaries will drop heavily and there would be a high need for these professionals. Along with the high-increase of taxes and long-waits as seen in Canada, UK, etc etc.

  • Transparency

    We need price transparency within the industry in order to protect the consumers and keep the trust within the healthcare system. Same with malpractice -- we need transparency in that regard and limit the repercussions for minor mistakes so, those who have made mistakes can come clean and upfront with the patient.

  • Prescription Drug Prices

    Drug prices are rising in the US compared to other places around the world -- I feel we need to lower the drug prices significantly and create some sort of agreement with other nations to get the lowest prices on drugs for the American people.
